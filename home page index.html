Reading Books :

How beautiful it is to learn to read and then translate this reading into a book that accompanies you and benefits from it and benefits others as well. Reading books is a fundamental reason for the progress of nations and the rise of books. It is food for the soul and an approach to it. Including reading books, reflecting on their meanings, and extracting useful and important information.

Some of the benefits of reading and reading books ;

  • Some studies have concluded that reading a lot protects the mind from many diseases, especially Alzheimer's disease, which affects humans at advanced ages.
  • Stimulating the mind: Reading helps to stimulate the mind, as it is an exercise for the mind that always helps it to internal activity and alerts it continuously to maintain focus.
  • Reducing stress Many of us get nervous, perhaps because of stress and constant thinking. To get rid of excess stress, read interesting and eventful stories to transport you from your world to other worlds.
  • Linguistic expansion and cognitive awareness of words Frequent reading develops your reading and writing skills and significantly improves your pronunciation of words and understanding their meanings and intentions with frequent reading.
  • Develop thinking skills The frequent reading of books stimulates inside you permanent thinking and solving issues within books, and this helps you to stimulate and develop your thinking.
